# PacketHero - Story

## Prologue

The story begins in a small and peaceful village.

The hero is a curious 12-year-old child who loves exploring and understanding how things work.

He has no knowledge of networking, computers or technology.

To him, the Internet is simply something that exists.

---

## The First Problem

One day, his mother becomes frustrated.

The Internet has not been working properly for several days.

She is busy and has no time to investigate.

She simply says:

"Maybe you can take a look. I don't have time right now."

The hero decides to help.

---

## Behind the Furniture

Following the cables behind a piece of furniture, the hero discovers several mysterious devices:

* A computer
* A strange box with blinking lights
* Multiple cables

Curiosity takes over.

While trying to inspect the blinking box, he accidentally touches a damaged component.

A sudden electrical discharge occurs.

Everything goes white.

---

## The Awakening

When he wakes up, something has changed.

The world looks normal.

But when he focuses on electronic devices, he sees strange glowing lines.

The cables are illuminated.

Energy appears to move through them.

He can see things that nobody else can see.

---

## The First Power

The hero has unlocked his first ability:

Physical Vision.

He can now see physical network connections.

Broken cables appear damaged.

Disconnected cables become obvious.

For the first time, he discovers that the Internet is not magic.

It is a network.

---

## The Journey Begins

After solving the Internet problem at home, word spreads through the village.

Other people begin asking for help.

A printer no longer works.

A Wi-Fi network keeps disconnecting.

A computer cannot reach the Internet.

Without realizing it, the hero begins a journey that will eventually lead him far beyond his village.

One problem at a time.

One network at a time.

One layer at a time.
